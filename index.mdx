## Typical workflows

- Write a prompt and generate a base image or video
- Enhance with upscaling, motion regions, or style transfer
- Export in preferred formats for web, social, or production

## Best practices

- Use clear prompts and include references to guide outputs
- Adjust parameters incrementally to refine results
- Save presets for repeatable styles and consistent branding
